---

The **Course Catalog** is SimCare's library of ready-made simulation courses. Each catalog course contains a curated set of AI client cases organized into modules — instructors can use one as the foundation for a class, and students can self-enroll in public courses.

## Finding the catalog

Click **Discover** in the top navigation, then select **Course Catalog** from the tab bar. The catalog is accessible to signed-in users and to visitors browsing without an account.

<Note>If your account is on a free plan, catalog courses will appear locked. Contact your program administrator or [hello@simcare.ai](mailto:hello@simcare.ai) to upgrade access.</Note>

## Browsing and previewing

Use the search bar and filters to narrow by topic, presenting issue, or difficulty level. Click any course card to open its preview, which shows:

- Course description and learning goals
- Module breakdown and the cases included
- Estimated completion time

## Using a catalog course (instructors & admins)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the course preview">
    Find the course you want and click **Use This Course**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set class details">
    Enter a class name and start date. The course content is copied into your program as a new class — your customization doesn't affect the original template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to students">
    Once created, add students to the class via enrollment code, email invitation, or LTI (Canvas/Blackboard/D2L). Students receive access immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>You can edit any course you create from a catalog template — add or remove cases, adjust due dates, or add your own custom cases alongside the catalog content.</Tip>

## Enrolling in a course (students)

If your instructor hasn't assigned the course directly, you can self-enroll:

1. Find the course in the catalog
2. Click **Enroll in Course**
3. Set your start date
4. The course appears on your **My Learning** dashboard

## Why a course might be missing

The catalog only shows publicly published templates created by SimCare administrators. Courses your institution builds privately won't appear here — your instructor will share those directly via enrollment code or invitation.
