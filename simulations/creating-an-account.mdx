<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to app.simcare.ai">
    Visit [app.simcare.ai](https://app.simcare.ai) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click "Sign Up"'>
    Click **Sign up** at the bottom of the login card.

    ![SimCare login page](/images/signup1.webp)

  <Step title="Fill in your details">

    - First name
    - Last name

    - Password (at least 6 characters)

    - **Continue with Google**
    - **Continue with Microsoft**
    - **Continue with SSO** (if your school uses single sign-on — type your school email and the SSO option appears)

  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete registration">
    Click **Create your SimCare AI account** to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing in with SSO

If your institution uses Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, or a campus SSO provider, you don't need a password — your school manages your login.

- Type your school email on the sign-in page
- If your domain is recognized as SSO-only, the form replaces the password fields with a single **Continue with {Provider} SSO** button
- Click it and you'll be redirected to your school's login

<Note>If you try to use a password on an SSO-only account, you'll see a hint suggesting you sign in with SSO instead. Password resets are also blocked silently for SSO accounts — your password is managed by your school.</Note>

## Magic-link sign-in

You can also sign in without a password using a **magic link**: enter your email, click **Send magic link**, and check your inbox. The link signs you in with a single click and works on any device.

## Joining a class

If your instructor sent you a class invitation link, click it to go straight to the **Join class** page. You'll either sign in with an existing account (and be added automatically) or create a new account that gets attached to the class on the way in.

## Two-factor authentication

After you sign in, you can enable two-factor authentication from **Account settings**:

- **Email verification code** — a 6-digit code is emailed each time your session expires (sessions last 24 hours). Recommended baseline.
- **Authenticator app** — use Google Authenticator, Authy, or any TOTP app to verify instantly. More secure than email.

You can also revoke all active sessions from the same screen if you suspect your account has been accessed without your permission.

## Signing in from a Canvas iframe

If you launched SimCare from inside a Canvas (or other LMS) assignment, you may see a banner asking you to **Open in a new tab**. Click the button — sign-in cannot be completed inside an embedded window. Once you've logged in there, return to your LMS and relaunch the assignment.
