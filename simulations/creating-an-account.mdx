## Step-by-step guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to app.simcare.ai">
    Visit [app.simcare.ai](https://app.simcare.ai) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click "Sign Up"'>
    Click **"Sign Up"** at the bottom of the login card.

    ![SimCare login page](/images/signup1.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Fill in the required fields:

    - First name
    - Last name
    - School email
    - Password (at least 6 characters)

    **OR** sign up with:

    - Google
    - Microsoft

    ![Signing up process](/images/signup2.gif)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agree to terms">
    Check the box to agree to the terms and conditions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete registration">
    Click **"Sign Up"** to finish creating your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
