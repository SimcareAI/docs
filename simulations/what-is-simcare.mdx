SimCare gives students a way to practice counseling by speaking with AI Client avatars — lifelike virtual clients that simulate real therapy conversations.

You can conduct full sessions with these clients anytime, from anywhere. No labs, no scheduling, no waiting for a partner.

![SimCare interface showing AI client avatar](/images/getting-started.webp)

## What makes it different?

### Over 500+ AI clients

Each one has a different background, personality, and presenting issue.

### Talk naturally

The client responds in real time — not scripted or pre-set.

### Practice on your own time

No need to coordinate with classmates or book a lab. Practice conversations with AI clients whenever it fits your schedule — whether that's in class at 9am or winding down at 9pm.

### Built-in feedback

Sessions are automatically recorded and evaluated, with feedback exported to instructors. You don't need to track anything — your instructors stay in the loop and manage grading and review.

## Why use SimCare?

**Traditional role-plays are hard to scale.** They require:

- Partners or instructors
- Scheduled time
- Recording setups
- Manual evaluation

It's expensive and logistically messy.

**SimCare makes practice simple.**

- You can engage with clients anytime, day or night
- No need to perform in front of others
- No risk if you make a mistake
- Feedback is automatic
- And the clients feel more real, because you're not pretending

<Tip>**Key takeaway:** This is a safer, more flexible way to build real counseling skills.</Tip>
