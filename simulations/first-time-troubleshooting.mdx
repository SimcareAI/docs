Running into problems during your first session? Here are the most common fixes.

## I can't hear the client

- Make sure your **speakers or headphones** are working
- Check that your **browser tab isn't muted**
- Try going back to the Discover page, click on the client again, and double-check your audio settings on the setup screen. Click **"Test Speaker"** on this page and ensure you hear a sound.

![Test Speaker on setup screen](/images/trouble_shooting_1.webp)

**If all else fails, refresh the page.**

## The client can't hear me

First, ensure you have **unmuted yourself**. Look for the microphone icon on the screen.

![Unmute yourself](/images/trouble_shooting_2.webp)

<Warning>
**Most common issue: browser permissions**

If it's your first time, Chrome or Edge will ask to allow mic access. If you clicked **"Block"** by mistake, follow these steps:

1. Click the **settings lock icon 🔒** in your browser address bar (top left).
2. Toggle the switches to **Allow** microphone and camera access.

![Allow microphone and camera access](/images/trouble_shooting_3.webp)
</Warning>

After fixing permissions, go back to the Discover page, click the client again, and double-check your mic settings on the setup screen.

## Nothing's loading / page is blank

- Use **Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge**.
- ❌ Safari and mobile browsers aren't supported yet.
- Try clearing your cache or using an **Incognito/Private window**.

## I can't log in

- Double-check your school email for typos.
- If using **SSO (Google/Microsoft)**, confirm you're logged into the correct account in your browser.
- Use the **"Forgot Password"** link if needed. You'll get an email to help reset your password. *Remember to check your spam folder!*
