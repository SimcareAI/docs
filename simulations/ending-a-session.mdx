When you're ready to wrap up, click **"End Call"** in the session window.

![End call button in session window](/images/end_call_1.webp)

## Wait for feedback to load

After you end the call, it takes about **30 seconds** for your feedback to load. Please be patient while the AI analyzes your session.

## Post-session tasks (if assigned)

Sometimes your instructor may ask you to complete a follow-up assessment, like:

- A problem statement
- A safety plan
- A treatment plan

![Post-session assessment tasks](/images/end_call_2.webp)

<Note>If these tasks are assigned, you will see them immediately after the session ends. **Once you complete the assessment, you can then view your feedback.**</Note>
