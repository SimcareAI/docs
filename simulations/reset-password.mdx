## Resetting a forgotten password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Navigate to [app.simcare.ai](https://app.simcare.ai) and click **Log In**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot Password'">
    Below the password field, click the **Forgot Password?** link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Type the email address associated with your SimCare account — use the exact email you registered with — and click **Send Reset Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Look for an email with the subject "Reset Your SimCare Password." It comes from `noreply@simcare.ai`. If you don't see it within a few minutes, check your spam folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the reset link">
    Open the email and click the password reset link. The link is valid for **24 hours**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new password">
    Your new password must be at least 6 characters. Click **Reset Password** to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Changing your password when logged in

1. Click your profile icon or name (top-right corner)
2. Select **Account Settings** or **Profile**
3. Find the **Change Password** section
4. Enter your current password and your new password twice
5. Click **Save Changes**

## Troubleshooting

**Didn't receive the reset email**
- Wait 5–10 minutes — emails can be delayed
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Make sure you used the exact email you registered with (common issue: using a personal email instead of a school email)
- Try adding `noreply@simcare.ai` to your contacts and requesting another link

**Reset link expired**
Reset links are valid for 24 hours. If yours expired, go back to the Forgot Password page and request a new one.

**Not sure which email you used**
- Check your inbox for any old SimCare registration confirmation email
- Contact your program administrator — they can look up your account
- Email [hello@simcare.ai](mailto:hello@simcare.ai) with your name and institution

**Account locked after too many failed attempts**
Wait 15–30 minutes, then use Forgot Password to reset. If you're still locked out, contact support.

## Signing in with Google or Microsoft

If you created your account using **Sign in with Google** or **Sign in with Microsoft**, you don't have a SimCare password — your credentials are managed by Google or Microsoft. Make sure you're signed into the correct account in your browser, then use the corresponding sign-in button on the SimCare login page.
