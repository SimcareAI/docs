Once you've logged in, it's time to start your first conversation with an AI client.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the clients">
    When you log in, you'll land on the **Discover page**. Here, you'll see:

    - A range of **AI client cards**, each with a different background and presenting issue (like anxiety or family stress)
    - Clients grouped by category to help you choose

    Take your time, read the short descriptions, and pick a client that interests you.

    ![Browse AI client cards on the Discover page](/images/first_session.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click "Start Case"'>
    When you're ready, click the **"Start Case"** button on any client card.

    <Tip>**Recommendation:** A pop-up will suggest finding a **quiet space** where you can speak freely. This helps make the conversation feel more natural and ensures the AI hears you clearly.</Tip>

    Click **Continue** once you're ready.

    ![Click Continue to proceed](/images/first_session_2.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the orientation card">
    Before the session begins, you'll see an **orientation card** with a short blurb about the client. This gives you key background information to help you prepare.

    ![Orientation card with client background information](/images/first_session_3.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decide on video (optional)">
    You have full control over your camera settings:

    - **By default, your camera is off.**
    - If you want the client to see your face, you can **turn your camera on**.
    - This is optional — totally up to you.

    When you are ready, click **"Start Conversation"**.

    ![Click Start Conversation button](/images/first_session_4.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Audio & video check">
    You'll see a video and audio setup page where you can:

    - Test your microphone
    - Preview your camera (if it's on)
    - Check your speaker output

    <Warning>**Browser permissions:** If it's your first session, your browser (Chrome or Edge) will ask for permission to use your mic and camera. Be sure to click **Allow** when prompted — otherwise, the simulation won't work.</Warning>

    ![Browser permission prompt for microphone and camera](/images/first_session_6.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unmute to begin">
    <Warning>**Important:** The client can't hear you until you **unmute yourself**.</Warning>

    Unlike other tools, **there is no "Submit" button**.

    - SimCare automatically listens while you speak.
    - The client responds naturally when you are done speaking.
    - You can also **interrupt the client** naturally, just like in a real session.

    ![Active session with natural conversation flow](/images/first_session_7.webp)
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once you've unmuted, your session has officially started. Start talking — the client is ready to listen.
