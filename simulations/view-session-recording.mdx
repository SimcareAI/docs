---
title: "View session recording"
description: "Play back your session with audio (and video if your camera was on) synced to the annotated transcript."
---

Every session is recorded automatically — audio plus video if your camera was on. The recording is part of your **Case Review** so you can re-watch any moment with full context.

## Where to find it

Open your Case Review for any completed session. The recording player appears alongside the **Annotated Transcript** section.

- **Desktop:** the player sits next to the transcript so you can watch and read at the same time.
- **Mobile:** the recording lives in its own **Recording** tab, separate from the **Feedback** tab.

## Synced playback

For most case formats, the annotated transcript is **synced to the recording**:

- Click any line in the transcript to jump the recording to that moment
- The transcript auto-scrolls as the recording plays
- Color-coded highlights from the AI feedback stay visible while you watch

This makes it easy to revisit specific moments — pick a line the AI flagged, click it, and see exactly what was happening when you said it.

## Group cases

Group cases use a dedicated group recording player that shows multiple avatars on screen, with the transcript split out so you can follow each speaker.

## Controls

The player includes standard controls:

- **Play / pause**
- **Seek** — drag the timeline to jump
- **Volume**
- **Playback speed** (where supported)
- **Fullscreen** (where supported)

## Privacy & retention

Your recording is stored securely with the rest of your session data. It's visible to you and — for assignment cases — to your instructor or anyone with grading access on the class. Recordings aren't shared with classmates and aren't used to train AI models.

## Downloading

Use the **Download Full Report** button at the top of the Case Review to export a PDF that includes the transcript and feedback. Direct video downloads aren't supported in-app today — contact your instructor or program admin if you need a raw video file for accreditation or research.
