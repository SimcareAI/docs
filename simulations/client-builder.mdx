The **Client Builder** (also called Patient Builder) lets instructors and admins create custom AI clients tailored to their program's training goals. You can build either a single-client scenario or a group case (family, couples, or support group). Once published, your client can be used in assignments just like any built-in SimCare case.

<Note>Client Builder is available to paid accounts (instructors and program admins). Free-tier accounts are redirected to the plan page.</Note>

## Finding the builder

From the Discover page, click **Create Custom Client** in the feature banner, or go directly to **My Cases** from your profile menu. You'll land on the Client Builder dashboard.

## Dashboard overview

The dashboard shows every case you've built or are in the middle of building.

- **Search** — find a case by name
- **Tabs:** `All Cases`, `Individual`, `Group`
- **Build New Case** button (top right) — opens a dropdown to start a new case

Each card on the dashboard shows the case's name, status of each AI-generated variant (prompt, voice, image, etc.), and quick actions.

## Building a new case

Click **Build New Case** and choose what kind of case to create:

- **Individual Client** — one-on-one therapy session
- **Group Case** — family, couples, or support group with multiple speakers

You'll land in the case editor — fill out the details, then save. SimCare generates the AI client in the background — this typically takes a few minutes.

## What you'll provide

For both types of cases you'll define:

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Display name for the client |
| **Demographics** | Age, gender, race / ethnicity |
| **Presenting issues** | What the client is seeking help for |
| **Relevant history** | Background context the AI draws on |
| **Training goals** | Specific skills or competencies you want students to practice |
| **Voice** | Preview and select a voice with style, accent, and age filters |
| **Image** | Pick an avatar image that matches the demographic profile |

Group cases add fields for each speaker (name, role in the group, dynamic with other members).

<Tip>Be specific with presenting issues and history — the richer the context, the more realistic and educationally useful the AI client will be.</Tip>

## After publishing

Once the AI finishes generating, the client moves from draft to **active** and appears in your case library. You can:

- Use it directly from your dashboard
- Assign it to a class — see [Creating assignments](/simulations/creating-assignments)
- Edit it any time (changes regenerate the affected variants)

## Troubleshooting generation failures

If a part of your case (prompt, voice, or image) fails to generate, the card shows a red error state with guidance:

- **Try regenerating again** — most failures are transient
- **Check your case details are complete** — missing or contradictory fields confuse the AI
- **Contact support if the issue persists** — reach us at [hello@simcare.ai](mailto:hello@simcare.ai)

## Sharing your case

Custom cases stay private to your program by default. To share a case across multiple instructors at your school, or to make it available program-wide, contact your program admin or SimCare support.
