The **Client Builder** (also called Patient Builder) lets instructors and administrators create custom AI clients tailored to their program's specific training goals. Once published, a client can be used in assignments just like any built-in SimCare case.

<Note>Client Builder is available to instructors and program admins. Students use the clients created here but cannot build their own.</Note>

## Starting a new client

Navigate to **Admin** (or **Faculty Simulations**) and click **Create New Client**. This opens the five-step wizard.

## Step 1: Basic profile

| Field | Required |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Yes |
| **Age** | Yes |
| **Gender** | Yes |
| **Race / ethnicity** | Yes |

These fields shape the avatar's demographic presentation and inform how the AI responds.

## Step 2: Clinical details

| Field | Required |
|---|---|
| **Presenting issues** | Yes — what the client is seeking help for |
| **Relevant history** | Yes — background context the AI draws on |
| **Training goals** | Yes — specific skills or competencies you want students to practice |

Be specific with presenting issues and history — the richer the context, the more realistic and educationally useful the AI client will be.

## Step 3: Image selection

Browse the avatar image library and filter by:

- Gender presentation
- Age range
- Race / ethnicity

Select the image that best matches the profile you built. The image appears during video sessions and in the case card.

## Step 4: Voice selection

Choose and preview a voice for your client. Filter by:

- **Style** — professional, casual, distressed, etc.
- **Accent** — regional and international accents available
- **Age** — younger vs. older vocal qualities

Click the play button to hear a sample before selecting.

## Step 5: Review & activation

Review all your selections. When you're ready, click **Publish**. SimCare generates the AI client in the background — this typically takes a few minutes.

Once generation completes, the client appears as an active case on your dashboard and can be assigned to students.

<Tip>Save a draft at any step — your progress is preserved so you can come back and finish later. Drafts appear in your dashboard under **My Clients**.</Tip>

## Troubleshooting generation failures

If your client fails to generate, the most common causes are:

- **Conflicting clinical details** — overly contradictory history can confuse the AI model; simplify and try again
- **Platform issue** — click **Retry Generation** on the client card; if it fails again contact [hello@simcare.ai](mailto:hello@simcare.ai)

## Assigning a custom client

Once published, assign your client like any other case — go to your class, click **Create Assignment**, and select the client from your case library. See [Creating assignments](/simulations/creating-assignments) for the full flow.
