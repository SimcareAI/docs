If you can't hear the AI patient during your session, try these steps in order.

## Quick fixes

- Make sure your **speakers or headphones** are connected and working
- Check that your **browser tab isn't muted** (look for a speaker icon on the tab)
- On your computer, confirm the system volume is turned up

## Test your audio on the setup screen

Go back to the Discover page, click the patient again, and when you reach the setup screen, click **"Test Speaker"** to confirm you can hear audio before starting.

![Test Speaker on setup screen](/images/cant_hear.webp)

## If none of that works

**Refresh the page.** Most audio glitches are resolved by a simple page reload.

If the problem persists after refreshing:
- Try closing and reopening your browser
- Use **Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge** (Safari and mobile browsers are not supported)
- Try an Incognito/Private window to rule out extension conflicts
