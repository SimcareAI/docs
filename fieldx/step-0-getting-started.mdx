Welcome to FieldX! When you first log in as a Program Coordinator, your field experience dashboard opens to the **Overview** tab.

![FieldX Dashboard Overview](/images/coordinator_getting_started-1.png)

## Your six tabs

Your main tab bar has six destinations:

| Tab | What's here |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | KPI cards, the term selector, the program-wide student table, and hours graphs |
| **Cohorts** | Create and manage cohorts (your class sections) — open one to see its members, assignments, and settings |
| **Sites** | The student sites, established program sites, and pending site requests waiting on your approval |
| **Assignments** | The forms and report templates library — build forms, group them, and assign to cohorts |
| **Run Report** | Run any of the standard FieldX reports (hours ledger, compliance audit, competency profile, etc.) |
| **Ask AI** *(new)* | Chat with an AI that knows your program data — ask questions, build reports, check compliance |

![Coordinator Sidebar Options](/images/coordinator_getting_started-2.png)

<Note>If you or anyone on the platform needs quick assistance, click the blue circle in the bottom right. The support system will try to help first and escalate to a human if needed.</Note>

## The Overview tab

Here's what the Overview tab looks like once you have students:

![Field Experience Page View](/images/coordinator_getting_started-3.png)

At the top you'll see three **KPI cards**:

- **Students Off Pace** — students whose hours progress is behind schedule
- **Average Completion** — average percentage of hours completed across your students
- **Pending Tasks** — number of action items waiting on you

Just below the KPIs is a **filter bar** with a search field, active filter chips (track, status, hours, clearance, activity), a **term selector** so you can scope all KPIs and graphs to a specific academic term, and a **More filters** button.

<Tip>If your program has students enrolled in multiple programs at once, a small **N tasks from other programs** chip appears here listing forms those students owe to other programs.</Tip>

Below the filter bar is a **student table** showing every active student in your program. Click any student row to open their full detail view (Overview, Insights, Sites, Timesheet, Learning Contract, Journal, Documents).

At the bottom is a graph showing hours completion per cohort and per student. Click into any cohort to see each student's progress toward their required hours.

![Hours Progress View](/images/coordinator_getting_started-4.png)

## Header actions

The top-right of every page has a few persistent actions:

- **Todos** — a dropdown with a count of pending tasks assigned to you. Open it to create, view, and complete to-dos.
- **My Program** — opens the program settings sidebar where you configure terms, tracks, clearance, members, notifications, and more
