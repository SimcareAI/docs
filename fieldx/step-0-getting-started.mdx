Welcome to FieldX! When you first log in as a Program Coordinator, your field experience dashboard looks something like this:

![FieldX Dashboard Overview](/images/coordinator_getting_started-1.png)

Your main tabs are **Overview**, **Cohorts**, **Sites**, **Assignments**, **Run Report**, and **Ask AI** — these are the most important areas for managing your field experience program.

![Coordinator Sidebar Options](/images/coordinator_getting_started-2.png)

<Note>If you or anyone on the platform needs quick assistance, click the blue circle in the bottom right. The support system will try to help first and escalate to a human if needed.</Note>

## The field experience dashboard

Here's what the field experience page looks like once you're in:

![Field Experience Page View](/images/coordinator_getting_started-3.png)

At the top you'll see **KPI cards** giving you at-a-glance data. Below that is an overview of all students currently in the field experience platform, with the ability to click into any pending tasks for those students.

At the bottom is a graph showing hours completion per cohort. Click into any individual cohort to see each student's progress toward their required hours.

![Hours Progress View](/images/coordinator_getting_started-4.png)
