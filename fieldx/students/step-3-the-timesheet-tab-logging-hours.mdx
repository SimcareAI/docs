The Timesheet tab is where you spend most of your time in FieldX. It's where you log hours, track progress toward your requirements, and manage past entries.

## Logging hours

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Timesheet tab">
    Click the **Timesheet** tab from your Field Experience dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Log Hours'">
    Click the **Log Hours** button (sometimes labeled **Add Hour Logs**) in the toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the entry">
    The modal asks for:
    - **Activity** — what were you doing? (e.g., Direct Service, Supervision, Documentation)
    - **Date** — when did it happen?
    - **Start & end time** — duration is calculated automatically
    - **Site** — which placement site were you at?
    - **Supervisor** — who supervised this activity?
    - **Cohort** — which class should these hours count toward?
    - **Notes** — (optional) additional context
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>The first time you log hours, you may be shown a one-time timesheet agreement you need to accept before the modal opens.</Note>

<Warning>You can only log hours after your clearance is approved. If the Log Hours button does nothing or shows an error, your clearance hasn't been approved yet. Check **My Dashboard > Profile Details > Clearance Tasks** for your status.</Warning>

## Tracking your progress

The **hours summary bar** above the table shows your total, direct, indirect, and supervision hours. Click it (or the **Learn more** link) to open the **Hours Summary modal**, which shows:

- Approved vs. unapproved hours by type
- Your required hours per type for your track
- Remaining hours and a progress chart

## Hour types explained

| Type | What counts |
|---|---|
| **Direct** | Face-to-face contact with a client or patient |
| **Indirect** | Administrative or preparatory work (documentation, charting) |
| **Supervision** | Formal supervision sessions |

## Managing past entries

- **Edit** — click a row's edit action to change details (can't edit approved/locked entries)
- **Delete** — remove an entry (can't delete approved entries)
- **Duplicate** — re-log a similar entry pre-filled with the same details

### Unlocking an approved entry

If an entry was approved and you need to edit it, use the row's **Unlock** action. If the entry is part of an active supervision report, you'll need to **Refresh** that report first before unlocking.

<Tip>Use the filter and search controls in the toolbar to narrow your view by activity, supervisor, site, cohort, or date range.</Tip>
