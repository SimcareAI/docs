At the top of your dashboard you'll find two tools to help stay organized throughout your placement.

## To-dos

Click the **To-Do** button to create reminders and tasks.

- **Personal reminders** — create a task for yourself (e.g., "Upload insurance renewal by Friday")
- **Remind others** — create a task to remind a supervisor or peer about an upcoming deadline

To-dos are a lightweight way to track anything that isn't a formal assignment.

## My notes (journaling)

Click the **My Notes** button to access the journaling feature. You can create three types of entries:

| Type | Who can see it |
|---|---|
| **Private** | Only you |
| **Public** | Your faculty can review it |
| **Supervision notes** | Documents specific supervision sessions |

Using notes regularly is a great habit — private reflections help you process your experiences, and public entries give your faculty visibility into your development.

## AI activity summary

On **My Dashboard > Activity**, there's an AI-generated summary of your logged hours for the selected period. It generates automatically once you have hours, and you can click **Refresh** to regenerate it at any time.
