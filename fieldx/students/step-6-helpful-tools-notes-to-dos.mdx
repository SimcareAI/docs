At the top of your Timesheet tab you'll find two tools to help you stay organized throughout your placement, plus an AI-generated activity summary on your My Dashboard to help you reflect on your progress.

## Todos

Click the **Todos** button in the header to create reminders and lightweight tasks.

There are two ways to use Todos:

- **Personal reminders** — create a task for yourself (e.g., "Upload insurance renewal by Friday", "Submit weekly log before 5pm")
- **Remind others** — create a task for a supervisor or peer about an upcoming deadline (e.g., "Supervisor needs to sign off on last week's report")

The Todos modal has four tabs: **Create**, **Assigned to Me**, **Assigned by Me**, and **History**. The header button shows a badge with the number of open todos assigned to you.

<Tip>Todos are lightweight and don't require any approval — use them freely to track anything that isn't a formal assignment. They're especially useful when you're juggling multiple cohorts or deadlines.</Tip>

## My Notes (journaling)

Click the **My Notes** button (the orange button in the header) to access journaling.

The Journal modal has two sub-tabs:

- **Journals** — your personal entries
- **Supervision Notes** — notes tied to specific supervision sessions

You can create three types of journal entries:

| Type | Who can see it | Best used for |
|---|---|---|
| **Private** | Only you | Personal reflections, processing difficult experiences, draft thoughts |
| **Public** | You + your faculty | Sharing your development, demonstrating growth, faculty check-ins |
| **Supervision notes** | You + your supervisor + faculty | Documenting specific supervision sessions and discussion points |

Use **Supervision Notes** for content you want tagged to a particular meeting — date, supervisor, and the notes themselves are stored together so you can revisit them later.

## Clearance Tasks shortcut

The **Clearance Tasks** button in the header gives you one-click access to your clearance status — useful even after approval if you need to upload a renewed insurance form or update a supervisor.

## AI activity summary

Your **My Dashboard** tab has an AI-generated **activity summary** that reflects on your recent hours, supervision patterns, and learning trajectory. See [My Dashboard](/fieldx/students/my-dashboard) for the full breakdown.

The summary auto-generates once you have hours on record. Click **Refresh** to regenerate it any time. It's a useful prompt for end-of-term reflections, supervision check-ins, or your own self-assessment.
