The Cohorts tab is your academic work hub. It surfaces everything that needs your attention — pending forms, reports waiting for your signature, and urgent items due soon. Think of it as your field experience to-do list, organized by cohort.

<Note>The Cohorts tab shows a blocked overlay until your clearance is approved. If the tab is locked, go to **My Dashboard > Profile Details > Clearance Tasks** and complete any outstanding items first.</Note>

## Inside a cohort

Click a cohort card to open it. Your tasks are organized into three columns:

| Column | What's here |
|---|---|
| **To Do** | Tasks you haven't started yet |
| **In Progress** | Forms or reports you've partially completed |
| **Completed** | Submitted forms and signed reports |

Most tasks will be evaluation forms or hour log reviews assigned by your faculty supervisor.

## Upcoming tasks panel

At the top of the Cohorts tab, the **Upcoming tasks** panel aggregates pending work across all your cohorts in one view. This is the fastest way to see what needs your attention without clicking into each cohort individually.

Items due within 3 days display an urgency flag — check this panel regularly so nothing sneaks up on you.

## Completing an assigned form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the form">
    In the **Upcoming tasks** panel or inside a cohort's To Do column, locate the form task card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the form">
    Click the task card or the **Open** button. The form launches in a new browser tab at `/assigned-forms`. Keep your main tab open — you'll return to it after submitting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete and submit">
    Fill out every required field and submit the form. Once submitted, the task moves to Completed in your cohort view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Need to make changes after submitting?">
    Look for a **Review / Resubmit** option on the submitted form. If that option isn't available, the form has been locked — ask your coordinator to reset the assignment so you can resubmit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Forms open in a new browser tab. If nothing happens when you click Open, check whether your browser blocked the pop-up and allow it for the FieldX domain.</Tip>

## Supervision reports

Reports (unlike forms) open inside the dashboard panel rather than a new tab. Pending reports awaiting your signature appear in the **Upcoming tasks** panel with an urgency flag if they're due soon.

You can also find all your reports under **My Dashboard > Profile Details > Timesheet Reports**.

A report moves through three signing stages: student signature → supervisor signature → faculty signature. A report stays **In Progress** until all required parties have signed.

## Learning contract

If your program requires a learning contract, a **Learning Contract** card appears above the cohort grid at the start of your placement. Here's the typical flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the contract">
    Click **Start** on the Learning Contract card (or **Continue** if you've already begun).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill it out">
    Complete all sections. You can save progress and return later — it stays In Progress until you send it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request sign-off">
    Click **Send for Sign-Off** to notify your supervisor. The contract is then locked on your end while they review it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enrolling in a cohort

If your program provides an enrollment code (common for elective or add-on cohorts):

1. Click **Enroll in cohort** in the page header
2. Enter the code your program provided
3. Submit to join — the cohort card will appear on your tab immediately

## Common issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Cohorts tab shows a locked/blocked overlay | Your clearance has not been approved yet. Go to **My Dashboard > Profile Details > Clearance Tasks** and complete any outstanding items. |
| Upcoming tasks panel is empty but you expect assignments | Your clearance may still be pending, or your faculty hasn't assigned tasks yet. Confirm clearance status first; then check with your coordinator. |
| Clicking Open on a form does nothing | Your browser likely blocked a pop-up. Check the address bar for a blocked pop-up notification and allow pop-ups for FieldX. |
| You need to edit a form you already submitted | Look for **Review / Resubmit** on the form. If that option is missing, ask your coordinator to reset the assignment. |
| A report is stuck In Progress | The report is waiting for another party to sign (supervisor or faculty). You cannot advance it — follow up with whoever hasn't signed yet. |
| Learning Contract card is not visible | Not all programs use learning contracts. If you expect one and don't see the card, ask your coordinator to confirm it's been enabled for your cohort. |
| Enrolled in cohort but the card doesn't appear | Refresh the page. If it still doesn't appear, verify you entered the correct code — enrollment codes are case-sensitive. |
| You don't see a cohort you were told to join | You may need to self-enroll using a code (see above), or your coordinator may need to manually add you. Contact your coordinator to confirm. |
