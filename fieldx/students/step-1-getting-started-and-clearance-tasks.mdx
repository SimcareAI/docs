## Why is my Field Experience page locked?

When you first click on **Field Experience** in the sidebar, you'll see a locked screen. This is normal — it means you haven't completed the required pre-application steps yet.

<Note>If you see tabs like **Discover**, **Presenting Issues**, or **My Courses** instead, you're in the SimCare Simulations area, not FieldX. Go back to the main navigation and click **Field Experience** to reach FieldX.</Note>

## How to unlock your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Clearance Tasks'">
    On the locked screen, click the **Clearance Tasks** button (or **Continue paperwork**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete each step">
    Your clearance steps typically include:
    - Uploading documents (e.g., liability insurance)
    - Signing program agreements
    - Selecting or requesting your placement site
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Once you've completed all steps, submit them. They'll be sent to your Site Coordinator for review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After your coordinator approves your clearance, your Field Experience page unlocks and you'll have full access to the platform — including the ability to log hours.

## Checking your clearance status

To see where you stand:

1. Click **My Dashboard** tab
2. Stay on **Profile Details** (the default view)
3. Click the **Clearance Tasks** card

It shows how many steps you've completed (e.g., "2 of 3 steps complete") or "Approved" once fully cleared.
