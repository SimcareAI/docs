## Why is my Field Experience page locked?

When you first click on **Field Experience** in the sidebar, you'll see a locked screen. This is expected behavior — it means your pre-application (clearance) hasn't been approved yet. Almost every action in FieldX — logging hours, accessing your cohort, viewing placement details — requires clearance approval first.

<Note>If you see tabs like **Discover**, **Presenting Issues**, or **My Courses** instead of a locked screen, you're in the SimCare Simulations area, not FieldX. Go back to the main navigation and click **Field Experience** to reach FieldX.</Note>

Once your clearance is approved by your Site Coordinator, the lock lifts permanently. There is no term-based re-locking — you only go through this once.

## How to unlock your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Clearance Tasks'">
    On the locked screen, click the **Clearance Tasks** button (or **Continue paperwork** if you've already started). This opens your clearance checklist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete each step">
    Your clearance steps are defined by your program and typically include:
    - Uploading documents such as liability insurance
    - Signing program agreements or acknowledgment forms
    - Selecting or requesting your placement site

    Work through each item in order. Steps may depend on previous ones being completed first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Once all steps are filled out, submit your clearance. Your submission goes to your Site Coordinator for review. You won't be able to log hours or access your cohort until they approve it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for coordinator approval">
    Your coordinator will review your submission and either approve it or request changes. You'll be notified when a decision is made. Once approved, your Field Experience page unlocks automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>You can upload or update your liability insurance at any time — even after clearance is approved — from **My Dashboard > Profile Details**. You don't need to wait for the clearance flow to handle insurance uploads.</Tip>

## Checking your clearance status

To see exactly where you stand in the clearance process:

1. Click the **My Dashboard** tab in the left navigation
2. Stay on **Profile Details** (the default segment)
3. Find and click the **Clearance Tasks** card

The card shows your progress (e.g., "2 of 3 steps complete") or displays **Approved** once your coordinator has cleared you.

<Note>**My Dashboard** has two segments: **Profile Details** (default, shows your clearance status, documents, and site info) and **Activity** (shows your hours breakdown and AI-generated summary). Make sure you're on Profile Details when checking clearance.</Note>

## What unlocks after clearance approval

Once your coordinator approves your clearance:

| Feature | Before approval | After approval |
|---|---|---|
| Log Hours button | Visible but does nothing | Fully functional |
| Cohorts tab | Blocked overlay | Full access |
| Hour entries and timesheets | Not accessible | Accessible |
| Placement site details | Locked | Visible |

## Common issues

**The locked screen shows but I don't see a "Clearance Tasks" button.**

Look for a **Continue paperwork** button instead — it appears if you've already started but not finished. If neither appears, contact your program coordinator; your clearance may need to be initiated on their end.

---

**I clicked "Log Hours" but nothing happens.**

This means your clearance has not been approved yet. Go to **My Dashboard > Profile Details > Clearance Tasks** and check your status. If all steps look complete, your coordinator may not have reviewed your submission yet.

---

**The Cohorts tab shows a blocked overlay.**

Clearance has not been approved. Complete and submit your clearance tasks, then wait for coordinator approval. The overlay lifts automatically once you're cleared.

---

**I submitted my clearance but haven't heard back.**

Check **My Dashboard > Profile Details > Clearance Tasks** — if it still shows steps in progress, your submission may not have gone through. If it shows submitted/pending, your coordinator is reviewing it. Reach out to your coordinator directly if it has been more than a few business days.

---

**I need to update my liability insurance after I've already been approved.**

No need to re-open clearance. Go to **My Dashboard > Profile Details** and upload directly from the insurance section there. It's available any time.

---

**I'm on a locked screen but I'm not sure if it's FieldX or Simulations.**

FieldX locked screen shows a lock icon with a "Clearance Tasks" or "Continue paperwork" button. If you see tabs like **Discover**, **Presenting Issues**, or **My Courses**, you are in SimCare Simulations — navigate back to the main sidebar and click **Field Experience**.
