## The Welcome page

The first time you click **Field Experience** in the sidebar, you'll land on the **Welcome to FieldX** page (at `/fieldx-get-started`). The welcome page shows three step cards:

1. **Complete Paperwork** — submit your clearance application
2. **Get Approved** — your coordinator reviews and approves your application
3. **Start Field Experience** — log hours and complete cohort tasks

At the bottom there's a primary CTA: **Start Paperwork**. Click it to begin.

<Note>If you've already started clearance, the page auto-redirects you straight to the clearance flow. You'll only see the welcome screen once.</Note>

## Why is my Field Experience page locked?

If you skip the welcome screen and click straight into a Field Experience tab, you'll see a locked screen. This is expected — it means your pre-application (clearance) hasn't been approved yet. Almost every action in FieldX — logging hours, accessing your cohort, viewing placement details — requires clearance approval first.

<Note>If you see tabs like **Discover**, **Presenting Issues**, or **My Courses** instead of a locked screen, you're in the SimCare Simulations area, not FieldX. Go back to the main navigation and click **Field Experience** to reach FieldX.</Note>

Once your clearance is approved by your Site Coordinator, the lock lifts permanently. There's no term-based re-locking — you only go through this once.

## How to complete clearance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Start Paperwork' or 'Clearance Tasks'">
    On the Welcome page click **Start Paperwork**. If you're past the welcome screen, click the **Clearance Tasks** button on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work through each step in order">
    Your program's clearance flow is a series of steps shown in a stepper at the top. Typical steps include:

    | Step | What you do |
    |---|---|
    | **Choose Track** | Select your student track (Internship, Practicum, Pre-Practicum, etc.) |
    | **Agreements** | Read and sign any required program agreements |
    | **Select Sites** | Pick from the established sites list or request a new site |
    | **Add Supervisors** | Add your site supervisor's name, email, and credentials (this triggers an email invite to them) |
    | **Insurance** | Upload your liability insurance form |
    | **Custom forms / quizzes** | Some programs add their own forms or quizzes at this step |
    | **Finalize** | Submit your application for coordinator review |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    On the Finalize step, review what you submitted and click **Submit Application**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    Your Program Coordinator reviews each application. You'll get an email when you're approved (or when something needs to be corrected). If you're rejected, the coordinator's note is visible at the top of the clearance page so you know what to fix.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After approval

Once approved, the lock lifts and your full Field Experience dashboard opens. You can:

- Start logging hours in the **Timesheet** tab
- See cohort assignments in the **Cohorts** tab
- View and manage your placement in **My Sites**
- See your overall progress in **My Dashboard**

## Common issues

**My clearance application looks stuck.**
Check the top of the Clearance Tasks page for a status banner. If it says "Awaiting Approval," your coordinator hasn't reviewed it yet. If it says "Rejected," scroll down to read their feedback and re-submit after correcting.

**I can't find my site or supervisor in the list.**
On the Sites step, click **Request New Site** to add a placement that isn't already in your program's established list. For supervisors, use the **Add Supervisor** form to invite them — they'll get an email and can complete their profile once they accept.

**The lock screen won't go away even though I submitted everything.**
The lock only lifts once your coordinator approves. Check the My Dashboard → Profile Details → Clearance Tasks card for your current status.
