title: "Step 5: My sites"
description: "View your placement sites, request new ones, and manage your supervisor connections."
---

The **My Sites** tab shows all the placement sites where you're doing your fieldwork.

## Viewing your sites

Each site card shows:
- Site name, location, and description
- Your supervisor(s) at that site
- Any site-specific requirements

Click a site to view its full profile.

## Selecting an established site

Your program maintains a list of pre-approved sites. Browse them and select one — established sites don't require coordinator approval, so you can often start logging hours immediately.

<Warning>Always confirm your site is approved before logging hours to ensure those hours count toward your requirements.</Warning>

## Requesting a new site

If your site isn't listed:

1. Click **Request New Site** (or submit during clearance)
2. Provide basic information about the site and your supervisor there
3. Submit for coordinator review

A pending site shows a **Pending** badge. Once approved, it moves to your active sites list.

## Canceling a pending site request

If you submitted a site request by mistake or want to change sites:

1. Find the site with the **Pending** badge
2. Click on it to open its detail view
3. Click **Cancel request**

## Adding a supervisor to a site

If your supervisor isn't showing up when you log hours:

1. Go to the **Timesheet** tab
2. Open the **Tools** menu (three-dots icon in the toolbar)
3. Select **Add Supervisor**
4. Enter their name and email

They'll receive an invitation and will appear in your supervisor dropdown once they join.
