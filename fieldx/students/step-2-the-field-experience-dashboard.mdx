Once your clearance is approved, the Field Experience page becomes your main hub. You'll see five tabs across the top:

| Tab | What's here |
|---|---|
| **Timesheet** | View your logged hours and add new entries |
| **Cohorts** | Access your class assignments, pending tasks, and learning contract |
| **My Sites** | View and manage your placement sites |
| **Run Reports** | Browse report templates scoped to your own data |
| **My Dashboard** | Your overview: clearance status, insurance documents, assigned forms, reports, and hours activity |

## My Dashboard

Your dashboard has two segments:

**Profile Details** (default) — shows your clearance status, insurance documents, assigned forms, and timesheet reports. This is where to check anything outstanding.

**Activity** — shows a donut chart of your logged hours by type (direct, indirect, supervision), plus an AI-generated summary of your activity. Use the period dropdown to switch between terms or view all-time hours. You can also export your activity to CSV from here.

## Where to find pending tasks

The best single view of everything you still need to do is the **Cohorts** tab — it surfaces all pending assigned forms and reports together, with urgency flags for items due within 3 days.

Your **My Dashboard** cards (Assigned Forms, Timesheet Reports) show the same items split by category, which is useful if you want to focus on one type at a time.

<Tip>If you received an email about a form or report needing your attention, click the button in the email — it links directly to that item. In the app, find everything pending on the Cohorts tab.</Tip>
