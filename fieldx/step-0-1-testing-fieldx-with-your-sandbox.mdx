When you first start with FieldX you'll be provided sandbox accounts. These let you easily switch roles and try things out as a Site Supervisor, Faculty Supervisor, and Student without affecting your live program.

## Your sandbox accounts

When your program onboards with SimCare, you'll receive credentials for:

- 1 Program Coordinator
- 2 Faculty Supervisors
- 2 Site Supervisors
- 4 Student accounts

## Recommended things to test

- **Clearance tasks** — Follow the student guide to see what students experience before they can log hours: [Getting started & clearance tasks](/fieldx/students/step-1-getting-started-and-clearance-tasks)
- **Create a new cohort** — Invite students and assign tasks: [Set up cohorts](/fieldx/step-3-set-up-cohorts)
- **Log hours as a student** — See how the timesheet flow works end to end
- **Approve hours as a supervisor** — Experience the sign-off process from the supervisor's perspective
- **Create and assign a form** — [Building forms](/fieldx/building-forms)

Most importantly — click around and explore! Try sending tasks, reviewing an evaluation, or leaving comments. There's a lot to discover and nothing in the sandbox will affect real students.

<Tip>After testing the platform, customize FieldX for your program's specific needs. See [Customization settings](/fieldx/step-0-2-customization-settings) to configure academic terms, hour requirements, and more.</Tip>
