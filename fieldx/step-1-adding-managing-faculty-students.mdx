All program members (students, instructors, and admins) are managed from the **Members** tab inside **My Program**.

## Opening the Members tab

Click **My Program** in the top-right of any page, then select the **Members** tab in the sidebar.

![Invitation Process - Select Members](/images/step-1-adding-students-1.png)

The Members area splits into two sub-tabs:

- **Students** — every student in your program with their track, term, clearance status, insurance status, and last activity
- **Instructors/Admin** — faculty and admin accounts with an inline role selector

Use the search bar, sort menu (Last Name, First Name, Email), and the **Show/Hide Insurance** and **Show/Hide Clearance** column toggles to focus the view.

![User List](/images/step-1-adding-students-2.png)

## Adding new users

Click **Add Users** in the top right.

![Add Users Button](/images/step-1-adding-students-3.png)

The **Add Users** modal lets you:

- Paste a list of emails (comma, semicolon, or newline separated)
- Or **upload a CSV or text file** — AI parses it so any format works
- Pick a **Default Role**: `Student`, `Teacher`, or `Admin`
- Toggle **Send credentials email to new accounts** — when enabled SimCare creates the account and emails the user a login link; when off the user must self-sign-up using the email you added

The modal shows per-email results when it runs (success / error / warning) so you can correct typos right away.

## Bulk actions

Select multiple students using the row checkboxes to open the bulk action bar:

- **Assign Term** — move a group of students into a different academic term
- **Assign Track** — change their student track
- **Assign Certs** — add or remove program certificates
- **Assign Role** — switch their role
- **Remove** — remove from the program (preserves their historical data)

## Per-row actions

Click any student row's actions menu for:

- **Create To-do** — assign a task to that student
- **View Profile** — open the full Student Details modal (Overview, Insights, Sites, Timesheet, Learning Contract, Journal, Documents, Run Report)
- **View as Student** — impersonate the student read-only for up to 30 minutes ([more →](/fieldx/view-as-student))
- **Remove** — remove from program

## Switching a user's role

In the Instructors/Admin sub-tab, use the inline **Role** dropdown to flip a user between Instructor and Admin without removing them.

## Pending invitations

Outstanding invitations appear in their own widget (Pending Invitations) at the bottom of cohort views. Each row has a **Resend** action that re-sends the original email.

## Supervisor invite emails

When you add a site supervisor (either manually or when a student requests them during clearance), SimCare automatically sends them an invitation email.

<Note>**The email comes from "SimCare"** — not "FieldX." Supervisors who search their inbox for "FieldX" won't find it. Ask them to search for **"SimCare"** or check their spam folder. The sender address is `hello@mail.simcare.ai`.</Note>

Key details supervisors need to know:
- The invitation subject line is **"You've Been Added as a Supervisor"**
- Clicking the link creates their account and gives them immediate dashboard access
- Once a supervisor has signed in, the invitation link expires — resending creates a new link
- **Re-send:** You can resend the invitation from the Pending Invitations widget in the cohort view. There is a **24-hour cooldown** between resends.

If a supervisor says they never got an email: ask them to search for "SimCare" in their inbox and spam folder. If they find nothing after 24 hours, resend the invitation from the cohort view.

## Recommended onboarding order for new programs

1. Invite Clinical Coordinators or Staff
2. Invite Faculty Supervisors
3. [Add established sites](/fieldx/step-2-managing-established-sites)
4. [Create cohorts and invite faculty supervisors to them](/fieldx/step-3-set-up-cohorts)
5. Invite students — either via direct email invitation (auto-connects them to a track and faculty supervisor) or by sharing the cohort enrollment code
6. [Manage and assign forms and reports](/fieldx/step-4-create-assign-tasks)

<Note>Next step: Set up your field placement sites so students can select them. See [Step 2: Managing established sites](/fieldx/step-2-managing-established-sites).</Note>
