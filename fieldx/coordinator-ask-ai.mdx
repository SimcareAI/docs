The **Ask AI** tab gives Program Coordinators a conversational interface to query FieldX data in plain English. Under the hood it generates SQL, runs it against your program's data, and returns results as tables, summaries, or exportable files — no SQL knowledge required.

<Note>Ask AI is available to Program Coordinators only. The tab appears with a **New** badge in your navigation.</Note>

## Opening Ask AI

Click the **Ask AI** tab in your FieldX navigation. A chat interface opens with a text input at the bottom and suggested prompts above it.

## What you can ask

Ask AI understands questions about your program's students, hours, cohorts, sites, and compliance data. Examples:

| Question | What you get |
|----------|-------------|
| "How many students are off pace this semester?" | Count with names and their hours gap |
| "Show me all students who haven't submitted a supervision report yet" | Filterable table |
| "Which sites have the most students this term?" | Ranked site list |
| "Export hours for all internship students to CSV" | Downloadable CSV file |
| "Draft a bulk assignment for all cohort 2025 students" | Pre-filled assignment template |

## Suggested prompts

When you open a new conversation, Ask AI shows several suggested prompts based on your program's current state — for example, "Who is behind on hours?" or "Show clearance status for all students." Click any suggestion to use it directly.

## Exporting results

Ask AI can generate exports in three formats:

| Format | How to get it |
|--------|--------------|
| **CSV** | Ask "export as CSV" or click the export button on any table result |
| **PDF** | Ask "generate a PDF report" |
| **Word (.doc)** | Ask "download as Word document" |

<Note>Excel (.xlsx) is not supported. Use CSV and open it in Excel.</Note>

## Managing conversations

Each chat thread is a saved conversation. You can:

- **Pin** a conversation to keep it at the top of your list
- **Rename** a conversation to something descriptive
- **Delete** a conversation when you no longer need it
- **Start a new conversation** to begin fresh (click the pencil / new chat icon)

Conversations persist between sessions so you can return to a previous query at any time.

## Bulk assignment drafting

Ask AI can draft bulk assignments for cohorts. Example prompt: *"Create a supervision report assignment for all Fall 2025 cohort students due November 15."* Ask AI will generate a pre-filled assignment that you can review and confirm before it's actually created.

## Common issues

**Ask AI isn't showing data I expect.**  
Make sure your Overview tab is set to the correct term — Ask AI uses the same term scope. Switch the term dropdown on Overview and try your query again.

**The response seems wrong or incomplete.**  
Try rephrasing your question more specifically (add a cohort name, date range, or student type). You can also ask Ask AI to "show your SQL" to see what query it ran.

**"Ask AI is unavailable" message.**  
This occasionally happens during high load. Wait a moment and try again, or contact [hello@simcare.ai](mailto:hello@simcare.ai).
