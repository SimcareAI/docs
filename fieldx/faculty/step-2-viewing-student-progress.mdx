---
title: "Step 2: Viewing student progress"
description: "Use the Faculty Overview dashboard to track student hours, identify off-pace students, and see pending tasks."
---

When you log into Field Experience, your **Overview** dashboard gives you an immediate snapshot of how your students are doing.

## KPI cards

The three cards at the top of your Overview tab show:

- **Students off pace** — students who are behind expected hours for the current term
- **Average completion** — average percent-complete across your placements this term
- **Pending tasks** — total outstanding tasks across all your students (not term-scoped)

<Note>The Off Pace and Average Completion cards only show numbers when a specific term is selected in the term selector (top right). If you see dashes (—), switch from "All terms" to a specific term.</Note>

## Student table

The student table below the KPI cards shows each student's hours progress, clearance status, and pending task count.

- **Search** — filter by student name, track, or term
- **More filters** — filter by track, pace status, hours bucket, clearance status, or activity
- **Eye icon** — click to open a student's full detail modal (Overview, Documents, Insights, Timesheet, Sites, reports)
- **Tasks button** — click the task-count badge on a row to jump straight to that student's pending items

## Cohort hours graph

Below the student table, a graph shows hours completion per cohort. Click into a cohort to see each student's progress toward their required total, direct, indirect, and supervision hours.

Quickly identify students falling behind and reach out before it becomes a bigger issue.

<Tip>To see pending tasks for a specific cohort — including reports that need your signature — open the cohort from the Cohorts tab and use the **My Tasks** sub-tab.</Tip>
