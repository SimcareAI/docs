title: "Step 3: Signing off on hours"
description: "Three ways to review and sign student hour reports: via the dashboard, via email, or via the weekly digest."
---

Approving student hours as a faculty supervisor is flexible — choose whichever method fits your schedule.

## Option A: Via the dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to FieldX">
    Open Field Experience from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find pending tasks">
    On your **Overview** page, click the task-count badge on any student row to jump to their pending items. Alternatively, go to the **Cohorts** tab, open a cohort, and use the **My Tasks** sub-tab to see all pending reports for that group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign">
    Open the report, review the hours logged, then click **Sign & Approve**. The signing uses DocuSeal — a secure e-signature workflow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Option B: Via email (no login required)

When a student submits a report, you receive an email notification. You can approve directly from the email without logging in:

1. Open the email
2. Review the logged activities
3. Click **Sign / Approve** in the email

## Option C: Weekly digest

Every Saturday you'll receive an automated email listing all outstanding signatures waiting for your approval. This lets you catch up on all pending approvals in one go without needing to monitor the dashboard daily.

## Signing as the faculty witness on formal reports

For formal supervision reports (e.g., the Weekly Activity Ledger), reports require multi-party signatures: student, site supervisor, and you as the faculty witness.

To sign as faculty witness:
1. Open the **Cohorts** tab
2. Click **Open Cohort** on the relevant cohort
3. Go to the **My Tasks** sub-tab
4. Find the pending report and click its action button
5. Sign in the DocuSeal e-signature panel

<Note>A report stays "In Progress" until all required parties have signed. Once everyone signs, it moves to Completed and a signed PDF is available for download.</Note>
