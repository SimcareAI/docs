---
title: "Step 1: Joining cohorts"
description: "How to join the cohorts your students are in, either by email invitation or cohort code."
---

As a Faculty Supervisor, your students are organized into cohorts (e.g., COU 690-002 or Spring Practicum). You need to join the relevant cohort to see your students and their assignments.

## Two ways to join a cohort

### Invitation

The Program Coordinator may invite you directly via email. Click the link in the email to set up your account and join the cohort. If you already have an account, you'll be added automatically.

### Cohort code

If your program provides you with a code:

1. Log in to FieldX
2. Navigate to the **Cohorts** tab
3. Click **Join Cohort**
4. Enter the code to join

<Note>Once inside a cohort, you'll see your specific group of students and any assignments related to them.</Note>

## Creating your own cohort

If your program gives you the ability to create cohorts, click **Create Cohort** on the Cohorts tab and enter a track code, name, and optional description.

<Tip>Next step: Once in your cohorts, review student progress from your overview dashboard. See [Step 2: Viewing student progress](/fieldx/faculty/step-2-viewing-student-progress).</Tip>
