As a Site Supervisor, your students are organized into cohorts — groups tied to a specific course or placement term (for example, COU 690-002 or Spring Practicum). Joining the right cohort is the first thing you need to do in FieldX: it connects you to your students, their assignments, and their submitted documents so you can review and sign off on their work.

You only need to join a cohort once. After that, your students and their tasks will appear automatically in your Overview and Tasks tabs.

## Two ways to join a cohort

### Invitation

The Program Coordinator at your students' institution can invite you directly by email. When they do:

1. You'll receive an email from **noreply@simcare.ai** with a join link.
2. If you don't have a FieldX account yet, the link will walk you through creating one.
3. If you already have an account, clicking the link will add you to the cohort automatically — no extra steps needed.

<Note>Check your spam or junk folder if you don't see the invitation within a few minutes. Emails come from noreply@simcare.ai.</Note>

### Cohort code

If your program gives you a cohort code instead of a direct invitation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to FieldX">
    Go to your institution's FieldX URL and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Cohorts tab">
    Navigate to the **Cohorts** tab in the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Join Cohort">
    Select the **Join Cohort** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    Type in the cohort code provided by your program coordinator and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once joined, the cohort will appear in your list and your students' tasks will show up in your **Tasks** tab.

<Note>Once inside a cohort, you'll see your specific group of students and any assignments related to them.</Note>

## Creating your own cohort

Some programs give site supervisors the ability to create cohorts directly. If this applies to you, click **Create Cohort** on the Cohorts tab and enter a track code, name, and optional description.

If you don't see a **Create Cohort** button, your program manages cohort creation centrally — contact your Program Coordinator.

## Common issues

**I didn't receive an invitation email.**
Check your spam or junk folder. Emails come from noreply@simcare.ai. If it's not there, ask your Program Coordinator to resend the invitation or provide you with a cohort code instead.

**The invitation link says it has expired or is invalid.**
Invitation links can expire. Contact your Program Coordinator and ask them to send a new invitation, or use the cohort code method to join directly.

**I joined a cohort but I don't see any students.**
This is normal if students haven't yet added you as their supervisor. Students must select you from the site supervisor list on their end. Once they do, they'll appear in your **Tasks** and **Overview** tabs. You can also check the **My Site** tab — if it's empty, no students have linked you to their placement yet.

**I entered a cohort code but got an error.**
Double-check the code with your Program Coordinator — codes are case-sensitive and may contain zeros vs. the letter O. Make sure there are no extra spaces when pasting.

**I can see the cohort but it shows the wrong students.**
You may have joined the wrong cohort. Contact your Program Coordinator to confirm which cohort code corresponds to your group of students.
