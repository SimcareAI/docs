The **Ask AI** tab puts a natural-language assistant on top of your entire program. It can read your students, cohorts, sites, hours, forms, and reports — and answer questions, build ad-hoc reports, and flag compliance issues in seconds.

## Finding Ask AI

Open the **Ask AI** tab from your main dashboard tab bar. It's marked with a small badge so it's easy to spot.

## What it can do

Ask AI is grounded in your program's live data. Examples of what it can answer:

- "Which students are off pace this term?"
- "Show me everyone in the Spring 2026 cohort who hasn't completed clearance."
- "Which sites have the highest pending sign-off counts?"
- "How many students are missing liability insurance in the next 30 days?"
- "Generate a Weekly Activity Ledger for Jordan Lee for the last 4 weeks and export it as PDF."
- "Build a table of every student, their site, faculty supervisor, and total approved hours this term — export to CSV."
- "Find any students with hour logs longer than 1.5 hours in the past 7 days."

When the answer is a table or list, Ask AI renders it inline and gives you **Export to CSV** and **Export to Google Drive** options.

## How it works

- Every query is scoped to **your program only** — Ask AI cannot see other programs' data
- Responses cite the data source so you can verify them
- Tables can be downloaded or piped into a Run Report template if there's a match

## Tips for getting good answers

- **Be specific** — "all students" beats "students" if you want everyone
- **Mention the term** when you care about a specific window
- **Ask for a format** — say "as a table" or "as a list" if it matters
- **Follow up** — the conversation persists, so ask "now filter to off-pace only" after the first answer

## Privacy

Conversations stay private to your account. They aren't shared with students, faculty, or other coordinators, and they aren't used to train SimCare's models.
