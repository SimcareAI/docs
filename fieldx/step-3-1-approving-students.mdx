title: "Step 3.1: Approving students"
description: "How to review and approve student clearance tasks before they can begin logging hours."
---

Once a student completes all their required **Clearance Tasks** (liability forms, site selection, supervisor contacts, etc.), their application appears in your approval queue.

## Finding pending approvals

A few ways to find students waiting for approval:

1. **Overview tab → Pending Tasks KPI card** — click to filter the student table
2. **Overview tab → student row** — students with a pending clearance status are flagged in the table
3. **Activity Log** (My Program → Activity Log) — filter by **Type: Application Forms**

You can also filter the Overview student table by **Clearance** status to see every student with submitted, pending, or approved pre-applications.

## Reviewing a student's submission

Click any student to open their **Student Details** modal, then go to the **Documents** tab. The full pre-application appears with every field they entered and every document they uploaded during clearance, including:

- Track selection
- Signed agreements
- Selected sites with supervisor contacts
- Liability insurance upload
- Any custom questions, forms, or quizzes you added

![Student Clearance Review Screen](/images/approving_students_1.png)

## Taking action

If the application is in `Pending` status, the Documents tab shows two buttons:

- **Approve** — grants the student full access to begin logging hours and participating in their assigned cohorts
- **Reject** — sends the application back. You'll be prompted to leave a note explaining what needs to be fixed

If the application has already been approved, a **Revert to Rejected** action becomes available — useful if you need to put a student back into clearance after a credential expires.

<Note>The student sees your specific feedback the next time they log in, so they know exactly what to correct before re-submitting.</Note>

## Bulk handling

For programs with large cohorts coming through clearance at the same time:

- Use the **Members** tab in My Program to sort students by clearance status
- Group students by track or term and review them in batches
- The Activity Log gives you a sortable, exportable history of every approval decision
