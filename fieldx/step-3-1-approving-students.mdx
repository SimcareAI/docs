---
title: "Step 3.1: Approving students"
description: "How to review and approve student clearance tasks before they can begin logging hours."
---

Once a student completes all their required **Clearance Tasks** (liability forms, site selection, background checks, etc.), they appear in your approval queue.

## Finding pending approvals

Two ways to find students waiting for approval:

1. **Overview page** — Click the **Pending Tasks** KPI card at the top of your dashboard
2. **Tasks tab** — Navigate to the Tasks tab in the sidebar to see all pending items across your program

## Reviewing a student's submission

Clicking a student's task opens a consolidated review screen showing every field they entered and every document they uploaded during clearance.

![Student Clearance Review Screen](/images/approving_students_1.png)

## Taking action

At the bottom of the review screen, you have two options:

**Approve** — Grants the student full access to begin logging hours and participating in their assigned cohorts.

**Deny** — If something is missing or incorrect, you can deny the application. You'll be prompted to leave a note explaining what needs to be fixed.

<Note>The student will see your specific feedback the next time they log in, so they know exactly what to correct before re-submitting.</Note>
