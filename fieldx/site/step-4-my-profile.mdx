The **My Profile** tab is where you maintain your professional credentials. Programs use this information for accreditation and quality-assurance compliance — it's not just a display profile.

## Opening My Profile

Click the **My Profile** tab in your supervisor navigation. The card heading reads **Professional Profile**.

## Profile fields

Fill in all four fields to be considered complete:

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **CV / Resume** | Upload a PDF, DOC, or DOCX file up to 10 MB. Drag and drop or click to browse. |
| **License type** | Select from: LCSW, LMFT, LPC, LPCC, LMHC, PsyD, PhD, Psychiatrist (MD/DO), LEP, or **Other** (typing a custom label). |
| **Supervision training** | Yes/No — have you received formal training as a supervisor? Selecting Yes reveals an optional details field. |
| **Years of licensed practice** | Enter a number. |

Click **Save Profile** when done. A confirmation toast confirms the save.

<Note>Your profile is visible only to you and your program's coordinators. It is not shared publicly.</Note>

## The profile prompt on first login

The first time you log in, a modal may appear prompting you to complete your profile. You can:

- Click **Go to My Profile** to navigate there immediately
- Click **Not Now** to dismiss and complete it later

The prompt will continue to appear on login until your profile is complete (all four fields filled). Completing your profile removes the prompt permanently.

## Updating your profile

You can update your profile at any time — click **My Profile**, make changes, and save. There is no cooldown or approval required.

## Common issues

**The profile prompt keeps appearing after I saved.**  
All four fields must be filled for the profile to be considered complete: CV file, license type, supervision training answer, and years licensed. Check that none are blank or missing.

**I can't upload my CV — the file won't accept.**  
Accepted formats are PDF, DOC, and DOCX. Files larger than 10 MB will be rejected. Try compressing your PDF or saving as a smaller format.

**I don't see a My Profile tab.**  
My Profile is a supervisor-only tab. If you don't see it, confirm you're logged in as a Site Supervisor (not a student or faculty account). Contact [hello@simcare.ai](mailto:hello@simcare.ai) if your role seems incorrect.
