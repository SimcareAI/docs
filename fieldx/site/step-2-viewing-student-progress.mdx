---
title: "Step 2: Viewing student progress"
description: "How site supervisors see their students' hours progress and find pending action items."
---

Your **Overview** tab shows all students across your placement site(s) and highlights what needs your attention.

## KPI cards

- **Total documents** — all reports and forms tied to your students
- **Pending signatures** — items awaiting your signature (Pending + In Progress)
- **Completed documents** — fully signed documents

## Student table

Below the KPI cards is a table of your students, sorted so those with the most pending tasks appear first. Each row shows:

- Student name and program
- Site name
- **N Tasks** button — click to jump to the Tasks tab pre-filtered for that student

Use the search box to filter by student name, track, or site name.

## Viewing a student's hour logs

To see the individual timesheet entries behind a report before you sign:

1. Go to the **Tasks** tab
2. Click a report to open its panel
3. Click **View Logs** to see each entry's date, type, site, duration, and notes

Any entry longer than 1.5 hours is flagged in amber — review these before signing.

## My Site tab

The **My Site** tab shows a card for each site you supervise. Each card lists the first few active students placed there, plus buttons to upload the site logo, edit site details, or remove yourself from the site.

<Note>If your My Site tab shows "No sites yet," it means no students have added you as their supervisor yet. Sites appear once a student selects you.</Note>
