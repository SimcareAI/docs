Signing student hours is done through the **Tasks** tab on your supervisor dashboard.

## How to sign a report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Tasks tab">
    Click the **Tasks** tab on your supervisor dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the pending report">
    Pending reports appear on the **Pending** status tab. You can also filter by student using the dropdown, or search by student or report name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the hours">
    Click the report row (or **Get Started**) to open the report panel. Click **View Logs** to see each timesheet entry — date, type, duration, and notes. Any entry over 1.5 hours is flagged in amber.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign & Approve">
    Click **Sign & Approve**. An embedded DocuSeal e-signature window opens — complete your signature to finalize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If you need to deny a report

Click **Deny** in the report panel. Before confirming, add your reason in the **Comments** section at the bottom of the panel so the student and faculty can see it. Then click **Deny** in the confirmation dialog.

A denied report moves to the **Rejected** tab and is not deleted. Faculty or a coordinator can issue a resubmit flow if the student needs to correct and resubmit.

## Status tab meanings

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Awaiting your signature |
| **In Progress** | In the DocuSeal e-signature flow; not all parties have signed yet |
| **Completed** | All required parties signed — download the PDF from here |
| **Rejected** | Denied — accessible read-only |

## Approval via email

When a student submits a report, you receive an email notification. You can approve directly from the email without logging into the dashboard — click **Sign / Approve** in the email.

<Tip>Every Saturday you'll receive a weekly digest listing all outstanding signatures. Use it to catch up on approvals in one session without monitoring the dashboard daily.</Tip>

<Note>Sign & Approve is disabled (greyed out) if a report has zero approved hours. Click View Logs to check whether hours are present. If no hours appear, the student needs to log and have hours approved first.</Note>
