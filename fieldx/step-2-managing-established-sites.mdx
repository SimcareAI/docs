Established sites are placements you've pre-approved so students can start logging hours without coordinator review. Sites are managed in the **Sites** tab on your main dashboard.

![Sites Tab Overview](/images/established_sites_coord_1.png)

## The three site sub-tabs

| Sub-tab | What's here |
|---|---|
| **Student Sites** | Every site your students are currently placed at and actively logging hours |
| **Established Program Sites** | All sites approved for your program |
| **Pending** | Sites students submitted that are awaiting your approval (yellow count badge) |

![Pending Sites Tab](/images/established_sites_coord_2.png)

Each sub-tab has filters for tags, site types, and status. Program sites automatically chip as **Active**, **Expiring Soon** (≤30 days), or **Expired** based on their affiliation agreement.

## Approving new site requests

Click any row in the **Pending** sub-tab to open the **Site Details** modal. You can:

- **Approve** — adds the site to your Established list
- **Reject** — rejects the request with a note for the student
- **Edit** — adjust the site details before approving

![Site Approval Actions](/images/established_sites_coord_3.png)

<Tip>If you want students who request an established site to be approved instantly, toggle **Auto-Approve Site Requests** on in **My Program → Settings**.</Tip>

## Adding established sites yourself

Click **Add Established Site** in the top right to open the **Add Site** modal. Two tabs:

- **Single Site** — manually enter site details (name, address, type, tags, contact info)
- **Bulk** — upload a CSV, spreadsheet, or text file with multiple sites at once

The bulk uploader uses AI to parse your file — no strict template required. Drop any reasonable list and it will extract the right fields.

![AI Bulk Upload](/images/established_sites_coord_4.png)

## Site affiliation agreements

For programs that need to store and track signed site affiliation agreements, FieldX includes a full DocuSeal-backed workflow:

1. Open **Field Experience → Affiliations** from the menu (or `/field-exp/affiliations`)
2. The page shows KPI cards (Total, Active, Pending, Expired) and a table of every agreement with its status, signatures, dates, and actions
3. Per-agreement actions: **Sign Now**, **Open in New Tab**, **Cancel Request**
4. Status updates flow back to the **Established Program Sites** sub-tab — sites with expiring agreements show **Expiring Soon** chips

Affiliation **templates** (reusable agreement documents) can be set up by your SimCare point of contact — contact [tiko@simcare.ai](mailto:tiko@simcare.ai) to get this configured for your program.

## All-sites map view

For programs with many sites, an interactive map view (with AI search and clustering) is available. Reach out to SimCare to enable it for your program.
