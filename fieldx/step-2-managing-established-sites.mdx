FieldX lets you maintain a list of established sites that don't require coordinator approval for students to begin logging hours. Sites are managed in the **Sites** tab.

![Sites Tab Overview](/images/established_sites_coord_1.png)

## The three site subtabs

**Student Sites** — All the sites your students are currently placed at and actively logging hours.

**Established Program Sites** — All sites currently approved for your program.

**Pending** — Sites submitted by students that are awaiting your approval.

![Pending Sites Tab](/images/established_sites_coord_2.png)

## Approving new site requests

When reviewing a pending site, you can **Approve**, **Reject**, or click **Edit** to modify the site details before approving.

![Site Approval Actions](/images/established_sites_coord_3.png)

## Adding established sites yourself

You can proactively add sites in two ways:

1. **Manual entry** — Enter site details one by one
2. **Bulk upload** — Upload multiple sites from any file format

<Note>The bulk upload tool uses AI to parse your data. As long as the information is clear, it works with any format — no strict template required.</Note>

![AI Bulk Upload](/images/established_sites_coord_4.png)

## Site affiliation agreements

For programs that need to store and track site affiliation agreements, this is a supported feature that requires a one-time setup from the SimCare team. Contact your SimCare point of contact to get this configured.
