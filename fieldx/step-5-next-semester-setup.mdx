---
title: "Step 5: Next semester setup"
description: "A three-step process to transition your program to a new term: create the term, archive old cohorts, and roll over students."
---

Transitioning to a new semester in FieldX is a quick, bulk process. You don't need to re-invite continuing students — just follow these three steps.

## Step 1: Create the new term

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to My Program">
    Go to the **Overview** tab and select **My Program**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the new term">
    Scroll to the **Academic Terms** section and add your new term (e.g., Spring 2026) with its start and end dates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Archive previous cohorts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open completed cohorts">
    Go to your **Cohorts** tab and click into a cohort that has ended.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Archive it">
    Go to the cohort's **Settings** tab and select **Archive Cohort**. Repeat for all finished cohorts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>Archiving does not delete data. It moves the cohort out of your active view while preserving all student hours, forms, and reports. You can access archived cohort data at any time.</Note>

## Step 3: Roll over students to the new term

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Members tab">
    Go to the **Members** tab on the main sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by the previous term">
    Filter the list to show students from the term that just ended (e.g., Fall 2025).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select all">
    Click the checkbox at the top left of the table to **Select All**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to new term">
    Click the **Assign Term** button that appears, then select the new term (e.g., Spring 2026) from the dropdown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your students are now active in the new term. You can proceed to create their new cohorts and assign them accordingly.
