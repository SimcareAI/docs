Cohorts are the primary way to organize students into groups — class sections, academic tracks, or placement groups. Each cohort has its own assignments, member roster, and faculty oversight.

![Cohorts Overview](/images/coord_cohorts_1.png)

## Creating a new cohort

Navigate to the **Cohorts** tab and click **Create Cohort**.

![Create Cohort Interface](/images/coord_cohorts_2.png)

You'll be prompted for:

- **Code** — a unique identifier (e.g., `SW-101-A`)
- **Name** — a descriptive name for the group
- **Description** — (optional) additional context

## Inside the cohort

When you click into a cohort, you get a focused dashboard for that group.

<Note>As a Program Coordinator, your main dashboard shows program-wide tasks. Cohort-specific tasks (like reviewing assignments for a specific class) are generally handled by the Faculty Supervisor assigned to that cohort.</Note>

![Cohort Dashboard](/images/coord_cohorts_3.png)

### Cohort tabs

| Tab | What's here |
|---|---|
| **My Tasks** | Tasks and oversight specific to this group |
| **Assignments** | View, create, and assign forms or reports for this group |
| **Members** | Students and faculty in this cohort — add or remove as needed |
| **Settings** | Cohort name, term, and the **Cohort Code** for student self-registration |

## Archiving a cohort at end of term

When a term ends, archive the cohort instead of deleting it:

1. Go to the cohort's **Settings** tab
2. Click **Archive Cohort**

Archiving hides the cohort from your active dashboard but preserves all student data, hours, and signed forms for future reporting and compliance purposes. You can unarchive a cohort at any time.
