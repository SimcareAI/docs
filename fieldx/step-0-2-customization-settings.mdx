Before you start adding students, take a few minutes to configure FieldX for your program. Click **My Program** in the top-right of any page to open the program settings sidebar.

![Customization Overview](/images/customization-1.png)

## The sidebar tabs

| Tab | What you configure |
|---|---|
| **General Information** | Program name, description, logo |
| **Terms and Tracks** | Academic terms, student tracks, certificates and hour requirements |
| **Clearance Tasks** | The pre-application steps each student must complete before logging hours |
| **Members** | Students, faculty, and admin roster — with bulk actions, term and track assignment |
| **Notifications** | Faculty daily digest, insurance expiration alerts, form due-date reminders |
| **Settings** | Program-wide behavior toggles |
| **Activity Log** | A searchable log of every form, report, timesheet, and page visit in your program |

*A **DocuSeal Templates** tab is also available to a small group of allow-listed accounts for building reusable signature templates.*

## General Information

Set your program name, description, and upload your program logo. The logo appears in the navigation header for everyone in your program.

## Terms and Tracks

![Organization Settings](/images/customization-2.png)

### Academic terms

Academic terms control how student progress is tracked across a semester. Each term has a start and end date and is labeled **Active**, **Past**, or **Upcoming** automatically.

Click **+ Add Term** to create one. You can have multiple terms running side-by-side for cohorts on different schedules.

### Student tracks

Student tracks define hour requirements for each kind of placement (e.g., Internship, Pre-Practicum, Practicum, Apprenticeship, PhD, Certificate). For each track you can:

- Enable or disable the track (this controls what students see in clearance)
- Set required total, direct, indirect, and supervision hours
- Choose **qualifying activity types** — granular control over which activities count toward each hour bucket
- Configure site requirements

The track editor is a 4-step modal: **Category Requirements** → **Hour Requirements** → **Site Requirements**, plus any extra steps based on enabled categories.

### Certificates

Set up **Program Certificates** with their own hour requirements (e.g., a Trauma certificate that requires N hours of trauma-related direct service). Certificates can be assigned to students individually.

## Clearance Tasks (Application Process)

The clearance process determines what students must complete before they can log hours. By default the steps are:

1. **Choose Track** — pick their student track
2. **Review Agreements** — read and sign program agreements
3. **Select Sites** — pick an established site or request a new one
4. **Add Supervisors** — provide a supervisor's name, email, and credentials (triggers the supervisor invite email)
5. **Upload Insurance** — upload a liability insurance form
6. **Final Review** — submit for coordinator approval

![Liability Form Upload](/images/customization-3.png)

### Customizing the process

Click **Configure Application Process** to add, remove, or reorder steps. Available step types include `Track Selection`, `Agreement`, `Site Selection`, `Supervisor Contact`, `Liability Upload`, **`Custom Question`**, and embedded forms or quizzes.

Click **Configure Agreement Requirements** to manage the agreements students sign — set which agreements apply to which tracks, mark them required or optional, and connect them to DocuSeal templates.

![Clearance Configuration View](/images/customization-4.png)

## Members

The Members tab is the canonical roster for your program. See [Step 1: Adding & managing faculty & students](/fieldx/step-1-adding-managing-faculty-students).

## Notifications

Toggle the program-wide email automation:

- **Daily Assignment Digest** — track faculty get a morning email summarizing what their students completed yesterday. Pick the send time and what to include (assignments, clearance forms).
- **Insurance Expiration Alerts** — students and admins get alerts at 30, 7, and 0 days before insurance expires.
- **Form Due-Date Reminders** — students and signers are emailed 7 days and 1 day before a form deadline. Only the person blocking the form is emailed.

## Settings

Currently a single toggle:

- **Auto-Approve Site Requests** — when on, students who request an established program site are approved immediately without coordinator review.

## Activity Log

A complete audit trail of program activity, with filters for type (Forms, Reports, Timesheets, Application Forms, Page Visits, Supervision Notes, Report Signatures), student, cohort, and date range. Export to CSV.
