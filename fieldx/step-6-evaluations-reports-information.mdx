FieldX makes it easy to visualize student progress and export data through the **Run Report** tab on your main dashboard.

## Standard reports

Your program comes with the following standard reports:

### Hours reports

| Code | Report | What it shows |
|---|---|---|
| **HRS-01** | Weekly Activity Ledger | One student's activities for a single week, signed by student, supervisor, and faculty |
| **HRS-02** | Multi-Week Hours Progress | Weekly totals per activity over a date range |
| **HRS-03** | Hours Trend Chart | Interactive visual of progress and activity distribution |
| **HRS-04** | Complete Hours Entry Log | Full chronological log of every time entry (PDF or CSV) — useful for audits |
| **HRS-05** | Hours Summary Snapshot | Total hours by activity type and supervisor |

### Supervision reports

| Code | Report | What it shows |
|---|---|---|
| **SUPER-01** | Weekly Supervision Compliance Audit | Per-student, per-week compliance grid |

### Site reports

| Code | Report | What it shows |
|---|---|---|
| **SITE-01** | Site & Preceptor Credentialing Report | Site and supervisor credentialing status |
| **QA-01** | Site Quality Assurance | Scores your field sites using site evaluation form data |

### Audit & pipeline

| Code | Report | What it shows |
|---|---|---|
| **AUDIT-01** | Activity Log Export | System audit trail of every action in your program |
| **COMP-01** | Student Competency Profile | Aggregated KPI/competency data from your evaluation forms |
| **PIPE-01** | Clearance Funnel Velocity | Student progression through pre-application stages |

## Running a report

1. Open the **Run Report** tab
2. Click **Preview** on any report to see a description and required configuration
3. Click **Run** to open the configuration modal — set the date range, student/cohort scope, and any report-specific filters
4. The preview modal renders the result; download as **PDF** or **CSV** depending on the report

Some reports require additional setup before they're available — those show a **Coming Soon** modal explaining what's needed.

The new [**Ask AI**](/fieldx/ask-ai) tab can also generate ad-hoc reports — describe what you want in plain English and it will pull the data, render a table, and export to CSV or Google Drive. It's the fastest way to answer one-off compliance questions without leaving the dashboard.
