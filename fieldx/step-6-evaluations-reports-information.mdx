FieldX makes it easy to visualize student progress and export data through the **Run Reports** tab on your main sidebar.

## Standard reports

Your program comes with the following standard reports enabled:

| Report | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **HRS-01** | Weekly Activity Ledger — one student's activities for a single week, signed by student, supervisor, and faculty |
| **HRS-02** | Multi-week hours progress — weekly totals per activity over a date range |
| **HRS-03** | Hours trend chart — an interactive visual of progress and activity distribution |
| **HRS-04** | Complete hours entry log — the full chronological log of every time entry (PDF or CSV), useful for audits |
| **HRS-05** | Hours summary snapshot — total hours by activity type and supervisor |
| **SUPER-01** | Weekly supervision compliance audit — per-student, per-week compliance grid |
| **COMP-01** | Student competency profile — aggregated KPI/competency data from your evaluation forms |
| **QA-01** | Site quality assurance — scores your field sites using site evaluation form data |
| **PIPE-01** | Clearance funnel velocity — student progression through pre-application stages |

## Running a report

1. Click **Run Reports** in the sidebar
2. Click **Preview** on any report to see a description and required signatures before running
3. Click **Run Report** to configure the date range, student/cohort scope, and other filters
4. Download as **PDF** or **Word (.doc)**, or print directly from the preview
