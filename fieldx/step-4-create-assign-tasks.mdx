Managing paperwork for field experience happens in the **Reports and Forms** tab on your main dashboard. This is where you create templates — once created, a report or form can be reused and assigned to any cohort or individual.

![Reports and Forms Tab Overview](/images/create_assign_1.png)

![Main Dashboard Interface](/images/create_assign_2.png)

## Reports: managing hour logs & signatures

A **Report** in FieldX defines the schedule for logging hours — when hours need to be submitted and who needs to sign them.

### Creating a report template

Click **Create Report** to build a new template.

![Creating a Report Template](/images/create_assign_3.png)

Configure:

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Title & description** | Give it a clear name (e.g., "Weekly Hour Log" or "Month 1 Summary") |
| **Signature workflow** | Choose who must sign: Student, Site Supervisor, and/or Faculty Supervisor |

<Tip>While many programs require a student signature, FieldX lets you remove this step if you only need supervisor approval.</Tip>

### Assigning a report

Click the **three dots** (⋯) on the right side of a report and select **Assign**.

![Report Actions Menu](/images/create_assign_4.png)

In the assignment dialog:

![Assignment Pop-up](/images/create_assign_5.png)

- **Select cohorts** — choose which cohorts receive this report
- **Set schedule** — set a due date, or enable **Repeating Assignment** to auto-assign on a schedule (e.g., every Friday)

## Forms: evaluations & surveys

Forms work similarly to Google Forms but are built specifically for field experience and are **fully HIPAA compliant**.

### Creating a form

Two ways to build a form:

![Form Creation Options](/images/create_assign_6.png)

- **Create from scratch** — drag-and-drop fields in the form builder
- **AI import** — upload an existing PDF or Word document (e.g., your current Mid-Term Evaluation) and AI converts it into a FieldX form automatically

### Assigning a form

Forms offer more flexible assignment options than reports:

- **Assign to groups** — send to an entire cohort
- **Assign to individuals** — assign to specific students, faculty, or site supervisors (ideal for targeted evaluations)

### Form settings

- **Repeating** — like reports, forms can recur on a schedule
- **Edit responses** — toggle whether users can edit their response after submitting

<Note>For details on building complex forms, see [Building forms](/fieldx/building-forms). For recurring assignments, see [Setting up recurring tasks](/fieldx/setting-up-recurring-tasks).</Note>
